New year music party at Giza pyramids

Join us on the new year party at the pyramids 31/12/2022.
a world class group of egyptian singers will make the night too special to be forgotten.
you are all welcome to this marvilhous night where the original history mixes up with the modern world.

2022.12.31 (Saturday) 9:00pm ~ 2023.01.01(Sunday) 2:00am

@ Giza pyramids - Al-haram st entrance.

Main Program

Introduction

The host will focus the light on the history of the egyptian music and its evolution through the long journey of history has starting from ancient egypt through the different eras till today providing some short audible sampels within 30 mins.

Egyptian Opera

The egyptian opera house is one of the oldest all over the world. And since Egypt has been open to its northern neighbours and at the same time has its own authentic and special music; the egyptian opera house has hosted numerous opera concerts which went out amazing and as a part of the party the opera team will perform at the pyramids. amazing mixture do noy miss it!

Egyptian Classics

A group of young egyptian singers will perform a very original and traditional egyptian songs orchastrated by a band of fine traditional muscians playing on traditional oriental instruments.
The show show will includ an amazing belly dance show on the tune of the traditional oriental music.

New Year Countdown

The countdown to the beginning of the new Year will include some festival atmosphere with fireworks and dj music with the most famous egyptian Dj star Mohamed Abdelsalam.
In addition to that, the music show will be acompanied by some visual shows reflecting on the sides and the front of the three pyramids.

Pop Stars

A group of the mos prominent pop singers in Egypt will perform anumber of their songs on the stage by the pyramids till the late hours of the night and this will be the eventual event on our program course for this magical night.
